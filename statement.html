 Portfolio

I am a studio practice art student whose art is mainly traditional. I have just started to learn how to handle technological art tools as a way to expand and grow my art skills. Many of my artworks contain fantasy themes, due to being interested in the genre when I was young. I had no way to do art online, so my only choice was to use either pencil or pen. As I started to draw more and more, I began to incorporate portions of my life experiences in my artworks(not directly; metaphorically).

I am very much an introverted person, yet, I do prefer going outside to enjoy the breeze and looking at the sky. As to why I like at the sky so much is due to the fictional creature called a dragon. A large lizard-like beast with its amazing scales and can soar through the skies with its enormous wings. It gives a feeling of freedom when thinking of those things in tandem with each other. Those what I mostly draw look like humans, they are truly creatures that blend in with modern human society. I can only do so much with traditional drawing until I need to express my characters in their fullest colors, which is why I am learning to do art online.
