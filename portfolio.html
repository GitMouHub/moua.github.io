Title, medium, year

This is an composite art piece. I started with a simple image of jellyfish and adjusted the colors’ tones to my liking. Then, I added another on top of the modified jellyfish image. I reduced the visibility of the second image to make it fade in with the jellyfish.

Title, medium, year

This is a glitch art piece done with some classmates. There were three of us, and we were each assigned to create an exquisite cyborg that would be glitched. Once the cyborg was completed, it was then taken into Audacity, where the image was represented as a wave. That wave was adjusted to making the original image look “glitched”, as it does here.
